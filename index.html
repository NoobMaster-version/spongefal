I'm an Electrical Engineering student at VJTI who loves building robots. I'm always excited to work on new projects - you can call me at midnight with an idea and I'll be ready to help! Since my first year of college, I've been making all kinds of robots and really enjoy seeing them come to life.

What makes me happy is when my creations actually work. I don't give up easily and will try everything possible to solve problems. This determination has helped me complete many robot projects and win competitions while learning new skills along the way.
