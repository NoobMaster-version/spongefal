Drone Warehouse Navigation - Eyantra

Project Overview

Participated in IIT Bombay's prestigious Eyantra robotics competition, where I developed an autonomous drone system capable of navigating complex warehouse environments. The project focused on precision flight control and intelligent navigation using visual markers.

The Challenge

The Eyantra competition required teams to build drones that could:

  • Navigate autonomously through warehouse-like environments
  • Detect and interpret ArUco markers for positioning
  • Execute precise pick-and-place operations
  • Follow optimal paths while avoiding obstacles
  • Complete tasks within strict time limits
